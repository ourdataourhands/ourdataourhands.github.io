Rise up!

Governments all over the world generate and store petabytes of data – for citizen access. Inspired by ClimateMirror.org, DataRefuge and many others, we want to change that.

We want to shield data from destruction.

Governments change. Priorities change. Sadly, science and data does not always occupy high regard. Public data in public hands can be shielded from politics and alternative facts.

We want data to be stored safely in every home across the planet.

The only way to compete with the vast amount of data storage that government has, is for millions of people to band together what we already have.

We want it to be easy to contribute to the storage grid.

Install an app on a phone. Or, on your computer. Better yet, buy a device and plug it in to your internet connection at home. Feel good.

We want humanity’s data available now. And, indefinitely.

The data should out live the politicians and their data centers. It should continue on, in every corner of the world. Forever.

What can I do to help?

Buy a storage pod

We're not ready to sell these pods just yet, but here's the idea:

  1. Buy a storage pod from us – small computer & HDD, ~$150 – tax deductible
  2. Receive it in the mail, plug it in – no setup
  3. Sit back and contribute to the grid – feel great!

Please join our email list and we'll let you know when they are ready!

Contribute space from your own computer

The operating system that will run on the storage pod will be Docker and Tahoe-LAFS based. As a result, our hope is that you will be able to run it in a container on your own computer.

Contribute ideas and code

We'd love to hear your thoughts, we'd love to have you contribute your skills. We're doing all of this in the open on Github, including running the non-profit itself.

Do the social media thing

Follow us, then tell your friends about us too! We're on Github, Twitter and Facebook.
